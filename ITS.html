Examples of Internet of Things (IOT)

Connected Car

A connected car is a vehicle which is able to optimize it’s own operation, maintenance as well as comfort of passengers and driver using onboard sensors and internet connectivity such as using the smartphone to control everything in the car. Most large auto makers as well as some brave startups are working on connected car solutions. Major brands like Tesla, BMW and Google are working now bringing the next revolution in automobiles.

Smart Home is the most searched IoT and the modern place to live in the future. Can you imagine we can switch off the fan or light after left the house using a smartphone? It may be benifits because the person does not need to use his energy to do his work. So this is the next level of internet and technology.
